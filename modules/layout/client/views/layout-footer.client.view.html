ABOUT DPITC

A One-Stop-Shop 'Convergence Hub' for inventors and technology users in the agriculture, aquatic, and natural resources sectors in the Philippines within every Juan's reach. It aims to connect all local agencies providing technology transfer and commercialization services in the country and to enhance the livelihood and business opportunities in the agriculture and aquatic sectors.
